Installation instructions

(This will be even more streamlined in the future)

  1. Download ATLauncher
  2. Once installed, log in with your minecraft account.
  3. Press the "Instances" button, and select "import" in the top left of the window
  4. Give it this .zip folder: Download
  5. Once it's finished, start the instance.
  6. Add server with ip: mc.dovas.se
  7. Have fun!

Mod briefing

(More details soon)
